# Wiki Tag Bank Highlighter

A RuneLite plugin that helps you organize your bank by **highlighting items or creating Bank Tag tabs using OSRS Wiki categories**.

Instead of manually tagging items, you can use the same categories found on the OSRS Wiki and apply them directly in-game.

---

## What This Plugin Does

- **Highlight items in your bank** that belong to a chosen OSRS Wiki category
- **Create a Bank Tag tab** containing all items from that category
- Works with **noted and unnoted items**
- Uses real OSRS Wiki category data

---

## How to Use

1. Enable **Wiki Tag Bank Highlighter** in RuneLite.
2. Open the plugin’s **side panel**.
3. Enter a Wiki category name  
   (for example: `Herbs`, `Mining`, `Food`)
4. Choose an action:
   - **Highlight in bank**
   - **Create Bank Tag Tab**
5. Open your bank to see the results.

Use **Clear highlight** to remove all active highlights.

## Example Categories

- Herbs  
- Mining  
- Food  
- Runes  
- Potions  
- Teleport items  

Any valid OSRS Wiki category that contains items will work.

---

## Plugin Settings

You can customize:
- Highlight color
- Highlight outline thickness

---

## Notes

- The **Bank Tags** plugin must be enabled for tag creation.
- Highlighting is only visible while the bank is open.
- Categories that include many items may highlight a large portion of your bank.

---

## Why Use This Plugin?

- No manual tagging
- No outdated item lists
- Uses familiar OSRS Wiki categories
- Fast way to organize large or messy banks
